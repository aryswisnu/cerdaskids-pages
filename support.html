CerdasKids Support

CerdasKids is a gentle offline learning game for toddlers (colours, animals, shapes, numbers, and daily routines). It works fully offline, has no ads, and collects no data.

Common questions

  • Does it need internet? No. The game runs entirely on the device.
  • Are there ads or purchases? No ads, and no purchases are enabled.
  • What ages is it for? Designed for children roughly 2–4 years old.
