Privacy Policy — CerdasKids

Last updated: 5 July 2026

CerdasKids is a game for young children. We care about your family's privacy, so we built it to collect nothing.

What we collect

Nothing. CerdasKids does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app has no user accounts, no sign-in, and does not require an internet connection to play.

Data and tracking

  • We do not use analytics or tracking of any kind.
  • We do not show advertisements.
  • We do not include third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.
  • We do not share any data with third parties, because we do not collect any.

Children's privacy

CerdasKids is designed for children. Because the app collects no data and has no ads, no external links, and no purchases enabled, it is safe for unsupervised play. We comply with children's privacy regulations (including COPPA and GDPR-K) by not collecting data at all.

Local storage

The app may store simple settings on the device itself (for example, progress preferences). This information never leaves the device and is not accessible to us or anyone else.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the date at the top of this page.
